论文笔记-论文常用高级词汇/句式

leverage.提高,利用

示例：we propose to leverage them also for supervising the few-shot learning process

novel.新颖的

示例：a novel module

propose.提出

示例：a new method is proposed

tailor.量身定制

示例：tailored pipeline

alleviate.减轻,缓和

示例：alleviate requirements/problems

aforementioned.上述的

示例：to deal with the aforementioned challenges

tackle.解决

示例：we develop a network to tackle few-shot segmentation
